Founded by Entrep Students.

Sarsa Co. is the culinary vision of a group of Entrepreneurship students dedicated to bridging the gap between traditional Bacolod flavors and modern convenience. Our goal is to redefine gourmet dining by offering slow-simmered, small-batch sauces that honor heritage while fitting the pace of contemporary life. We envision a future where student-driven innovation empowers local culinary traditions to reach every Filipino home.

Guidance for the discerning palate.

How is the Reserve packaged?
Our sauces are blast-frozen in premium vacuum packs to lock in peak flavor. This ensures that the artisanal quality is preserved from our boutique kitchen to yours. Simply reheat and enjoy.
What defines this Reserve?
It is the rejection of the ordinary. We use only top-tier organic ingredients, strictly following traditional methods with zero preservatives.
Pairing Suggestions
Our Spaghetti Sauce excels with long pastas like linguine, while the Carbonara is best matched with artisanal fettuccine or rigatoni.
Order Processing Time
As our batches are small and artisanal, please allow 2-3 business days for preparation and secure transit to your kitchen.
Shipping Policy
We offer complimentary white-glove delivery within Bacolod City. For outside Bacolod orders, our team will contact you regarding your order and delivery.
Refund Policy
Due to the artisanal and perishable nature of our Reserve batches, we cannot accept returns once opened. However, if your delivery is compromised, a replacement batch will be dispatched immediately.
Privacy Policy
Your privacy is treated with the same respect as our traditions. Personal data is utilized exclusively for order fulfillment and boutique communications. We never share your details with external entities.
Accessibility Statement
We are dedicated to ensuring a seamless digital experience for all connoisseurs. If you encounter any accessibility barriers, our boutique concierge is available to assist at concierge@sarsa.co.
